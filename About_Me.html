Originally a native of Wisconsin, I moved to Colorado to attend Colorado College.  Once I was here, I realized I was unwilling to leave such a beautiful state. After graduating with degrees in Art History and French Literature, I worked as a Development Officer for my alma mater, raising much needed money to support scholarships and keep the school running.
