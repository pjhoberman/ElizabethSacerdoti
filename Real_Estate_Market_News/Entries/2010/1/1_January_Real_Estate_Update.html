January Real Estate Update

 

Real Estate Market in Review

Since the market’s underperformance early in 2009, the last nine months have actually seen an increase in pending home sales.  In Denver, for example, housing sales surged 23% in November alone over the same month in 2008.  The market is starting to stabilize and consumer confidence has been growing.  With the federal government stepping in with a wide variety of laws that will help consumers understand more about their mortgages and help people stay in their homes, real estate professionals are being held to a higher standard.  If you haven’t bought or sold a home in the last three years, I think when you return to the market, you will notice a positive change in contracts, disclosures and service.

Tax Credit- Running Out!

Whether you are an existing homeowner or a first time buyer, this tax credit really is a fabulous opportunity to make a move!  In order for you to qualify for the tax credit, you must buy a primary residence and you must be under contract on the property by April 30th, 2010.  Additionally, the income limit recently doubled, so even if you weren’t a candidate before, you may be now.  You don’t have to close on the house until June 30th--however, get out there and start looking if you want to take advantage of the $8,000 tax credit.  Please email me directly for more specifics on the eligibility requirements.

Short Sales and Foreclosures- Expert Advice

Short Sales, a word most people didn’t know a year ago, means when the bank agrees to sell a home for less money than the balance on the current loan.  With short sales and foreclosures representing a significant percentage of the market share of most neighborhoods, now is a critical time to be informed about how these properties differ from traditional sales.  As a result, I have recently become certified as a Short Sale and Foreclosure Resource.  If you have been thinking about investing in a short sale or are if you are interested in your options should you be behind in a mortgage payment, I’m available to answer your questions.  As of April 2010, new federal laws will be put in place in an effort to streamline the time-consuming short sale process.

Happy New Year!

For many people, the New Year is the perfect time to make plans for the coming year.  If your plans include thinking about buying or selling a home, consider contacting me in advance to talk about your situation.  Being prepared and knowledgeable about your real estate market is one of the easiest and best ways to sell a house or find the house of your dreams.

Property Up Close: Villa in the heart of Provence


“La Fleur de Lys” is a spectacular Provençal villa overlooking the Meditteranean Sea in the town of La Croix Valmer, France.  Located a mere fifteen minutes from the famed port town of St. Tropez, this home is situated in the heart of the very best part of Provence. 

 

Enclosed by private electric gates, this 3 bedroom, 3 bath home also has additional sleeping space to accommodate up to 12 adults.  The home is fully equipped with modern conveniences ranging from an American style kitchen to wireless internet and Sky TV.  A large sliding glass door in the dining room can be pulled back into the wall, opening up the entire house to the large terraced patio.

 

The large lot features a state of the art, heated illusion pool with a panoramic view of the sea, many levels of tiled terraces, a double-sized boules court and fabulous views of the Meditteranean Sea and surrounding vineyards.  The garden, on an automatic watering system, has been beautifully landscaped with old olive trees, palm trees and exposed levels of an antique stone wall.
