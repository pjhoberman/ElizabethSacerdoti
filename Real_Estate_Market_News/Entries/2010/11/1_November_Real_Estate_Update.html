Investment Suggestion: Buy a Vacation Rental

I’m going to go ahead and say it again: real estate is still a solid long term investment and it’s never been a better time to buy.  But how can you take advantage?  Purchase a vacation home and rent it out to travelers.  Rental properties in vacation markets tend to pay for themselves and you can enjoy the benefits while earning a profit.  Many vacation markets have never had so much inventory, prices are unlikely to ever be better and interest rates are still unbeatable. Its also never been easier to rent out properties with websites like VRBO.com and craigslist drawing records visitors to their sites.


Latest News on the Foreclosure Situation

With the announcement of faulty “robo-signing” of foreclosure documents, many banks are temporarily freezing their foreclosures in order to make sure their paperwork is correct.  What does this mean to you?  A halt of new foreclosures from entering the market could provide an upswing in real estate as their will be fewer homes for sale.  Long term, seeing these troubled properties through the market will be the only way to full heal the real estate industry, so we’ve still got some time before the market is more fully balanced.


Best cities for trick or treating?

Zillow announced the top 20 cities for trick or treating based on walk score, population density, crime rates and home values.  This year, Denver ranked 5th!  The top four cities include Seattle, San Francisco, Portland and San Jose.
