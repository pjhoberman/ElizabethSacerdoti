Lending News: Hold off on the new car

Lenders are now pulling credit scores very close to the day of closing.  As a result, if there is a change in the debt to income ratio due to a large purchase made prior to closing, it could jeopardize your ability to close on the home.  Be careful and always speak to your realtor and lender if you are worried about this issue or have questions.

What’s Going on with the Market?

Well, the preliminary results are in and even though the home purchase tax credit has ended, we’ve still seen lots of activity.  My company is up in production about 50% over last year this time due to the incredible mortgage rates and inventory out there.  I did notice a price drop in many homes in the aftermath of the expiration of the tax credit, but things are definitely moving along and great properties don’t last long.  The market is balancing out in Denver, and realtors in general are working harder and longer to get deals to close due to tightened lending laws and inspections, but in the long run, it will only be a benefit to the consumer.
