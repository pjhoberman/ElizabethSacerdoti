House Maintenance To-Do's


As we get into June, home maintenance projects begin to re-appear on many people’s calendars.  At the request of some clients, I’ve decided to write my own version of a home maintenance to do list.  These are some suggestions that I thought of today and I hope it helps you all!


Day of Closing

Call Excel to transfer utilities

Water/Sewer is automatically transferred by title company


When You Move In

Paint before you move in any furniture!

Rekey or Change your locks

Set up Alarm System

Set up Phone, Internet, TV

Change your mailing address with post office and all of your bills and friends!!

Set up electronic payment of mortgage

Set up house account for joint home purchases

Get a safety deposit box for house documents

Change Garage Door Opener Code

Set up iPhone alarms on garbage, recycling and street sweeping


Every 3 Months:

Change/Clean your furnace Filter

Use self cleaning oven feature


Every Year:

Service your Furnace and Air Conditioning System

Clean your sewer line

Check batteries in smoke/CO2 detectors

Renew Alarm with City of Denver

Make sure garage door safety reverse is functioning

Ensure all GFCI outlets are working

Clean fridge coils

Ensure Vent for Dryer is clear

Re-caulk any tubs, sinks where seal is broken

Clean out window wells in basement

Clean dishwasher food filter


Every October 15th

Winterize your sprinkler system

Check weather stripping, window and door seal


Every May 15th

Turn on your sprinkler system

Aerate the lawn


Every 3-5 Years

Clean Windows

Clean out Gutters

Check Trim for painting

Clean your air ducts

Clean Chimneys

Drain water heater to remove sediment (usually 1-2 gallons)

Trim trees

Clean Carpet
