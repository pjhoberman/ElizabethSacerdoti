Condos

When buying a condo, realtor’s have a much tougher job for their clients than ever before.  This is in part due to the mountains of information they must review to ensure the condo development has FHA approval.  Even if you don’t get an FHA loan, this will certainly affect your ability to sell down the road, so make sure you know ALL the facts before buying a condo.  I can help with that : )  For more information about this, check out this Denver Post article: Article on FHA Approval and Condos
