The New Real Estate Market

Multiple Offers?  Back Up Offers?  Selling above asking price?  It’s happening, people.  No one has ever seen a market turn so suddenly and there’s good news on both sides.  For sellers, well priced, well staged homes are selling often in a matter of days and increasingly close to or even above the asking price.  For buyers, you’re all very knowledgeable about value in this market, and that’s why the best priced properties are selling fast- but they are also often appraising above that!   You’re getting the value on two ends- one- prices are still low in Denver and two- you’ve got a great mortgage rate.  Even sellers who may sell  at a loss are starting to list their homes so they can recover any lost profit back on their next home.  This is a unique market and one we hope will continue through the summer.   It’s great to see a healthy and balanced real estate market in Denver!


Property Insurance- New Problems

Most people hear me say at some point, “Be sure to get a quote for your homeowner’s insurance by (insert date).”  I know with the zillion things you’re already doing, this can easily fall to the back burner.  However, in the ever-changing world of real estate, this deadline is an important one.  Insurance companies are starting to really analyze homes and make sure claims don’t pop up early on.  What this means is that the roof that your inspector said might be a problem- might actually jeopardize your insurance- so get it checked out early in the deal!
