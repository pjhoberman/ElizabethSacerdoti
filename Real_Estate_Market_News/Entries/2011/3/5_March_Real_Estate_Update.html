FHA Loans- Major Change Coming!


The monthly mortgage insurance premium required for FHA loans will undergo a significant increase (.25%) for loans given case numbers after April 18, 2011.  To put it in perspective, if your loan will be valued at $250,000, this will mean your monthly payment will increase over $50 a month! For some, this will mean that a conventional loan with mortgage insurance will be a better way to go.  You may want to consider changing your timeline to purchase if you had considered making a purchase this spring.


The Denver Market in review


Homes sales have now been exceeding new inventory for several weeks in both the condo and single family market.  Market prices have not yet moved higher as excess inventory is moved as it has still been a buyer’s market.  However, as this trend continues, we will likely see upward pressure on pricing.  Currently, the average days on market for a home is 149, for a condo, 181 days.  In February in the entire Denver metro area, 1770 homes and 459 condos were sold.
