The Denver Market in Review:

We’ve been seeing mostly bearish activity in the market with days on market once again on the rise and prices trending downward for single family homes.  Incredibly, those homes that are put on the market that show well and are priced correctly are still selling in less than 30 days, often with competing offers.  Buyers recognize value more than ever before and will compete to get it. For condos, prices have remained more stable but as inventory continues to grow, we’ll see some downward pressure on prices in the coming months. 

Loans continue to be very challenging to work through, so more time spent with a lender prior to house hunting is absolutely required.  This also means finding the right lender that can provide the level of service to bring the loan to fruition.

This level of affordability has been unprecedented in the last 20 years of real estate.  I can’t stress enough, NOW is the time to buy.
